The Story

The Videos

In 2023, I discovered Ray's album Wrinkle In The Plan. I had been out of contact with Ray for literally 50 years, ever since the days when we were both playing in a San Francisco band called The Brothers And. We spent about three years making music — eventually leading to the release of Ray's song, Ain't Gonna Kill the Spirit, with national airplay and a near-miss at the big time. Hearing Ray's voice and unfettered, authentic personality on his new originals after all those years touched me deeply, and it motivated me to create some videos to bring Ray's songs to life visually. After chatting with Ray online, I added some sax and flute solos and horn parts to flesh out his tracks. I hope we can bring more of his new material to life in this way. The true voice of Ray White is a bright light in troubled times.

Ray Shirley White is a singer and guitarist whose voice became central to some of the most adventurous music of the 1970s and 80s. Born in the American south, his journey took him through the heart of America and its music before landing him on stage with Frank Zappa, where he became a featured performer across some of Zappa's most celebrated touring years.

But Ray has always been someone else: a thoughtful songwriter with a unique voice of his own. The songs on his album Wrinkle In The Plan — soulful, warm, politically awake, and deeply personal — represent an artist who never stopped growing. These videos exist to bring those songs the visual life they deserve.
